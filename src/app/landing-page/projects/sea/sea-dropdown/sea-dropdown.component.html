Ports Protection with multi-subsurface sensors integrated with Coastal Radar and Camera assets

Extending the “Wet Fence” underwater Unattended underwater sensors dramatically improve the range and capability of the virtual “wet fence”. Every hour of the day, every day, in total darkness and in harsh weather, CITC underwater arrays safeguard shorelines and harbors from intrusions as small as a single swimmer or diver. Additionally, such systems can be utilized to assist in man-overboard situations and find hazards such as underwater wreckage.
