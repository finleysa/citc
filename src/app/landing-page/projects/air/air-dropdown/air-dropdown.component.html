CITC currently supports three airborne intelligence lines of operation:

  • IMINT – direct visual observation of the target area with visible-wavelength cameras, infrared and spectral imagers, and full-motion video
  • COMINT – GSM/2G intercepts and decryption, MITM attacks, IMSI catcher, pattern-of-life social networking from mobile phone collections
  • ELINT – RF scanning, signal decoding, direction finding
