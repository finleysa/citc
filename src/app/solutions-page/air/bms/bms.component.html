BMS - DATA LINK

  • EnerLinksIII data link by BMS, includes:
    • HeliCoder-4 Transmitter
    • DLC50 Downlink Control Panel
    • DiversaPod ground antenna
    • DR6000 Mark II Ground Station Receiver
    • BDEC-2 Ground Station Decoder
  • Pushes video, audio and IP traffic to the ground
  • Integrated H.264 compression HD video
  • Data multiplexing of compressed video, IP traffic, metadata, and serial port data
  • Low Probability of Intercept (LPI) combined with AES-256 encryption
  • Power consumption under 50 watts
  • 5 Mbps Modem
  • MISB compliant metadata handling (0604.1, 0601.4)
  • Half-duplex with option for full-duplex
  • Operates in L, S and C bands
  • Calculated LOS range is 75 nmi at 5 Mbps with 10 dB of link margin for multipath fading
  • Fully integrated ground receiving stations
